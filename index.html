04 · Middleman

Trusted Transactions

I've been operating as a trusted middleman for 3+ years, and trust is something I've earned, not assumed. I got my start as an official trusted middleman for Punch Simulator (500M+ visits), where I handled a high volume of tickets to facilitate safe gamepass and pet trades between players. It was a role that taught me the importance of precision, neutrality, and speed.

Since then, my middleman work has expanded beyond Punch Simulator to other games including Farmer Simulator, Unbox Your Car, Unbox a Factory, and more. No matter the context, my approach stays the same: clear process, no shortcuts, and the transaction doesn't move until both parties are protected.

I've accumulated numerous vouches across trust-critical games like Island and Bee Swarm Simulator, communities where your reputation is everything and a single bad act can define you permanently. Mine has stayed clean throughout, and that's something I'm genuinely proud of.
